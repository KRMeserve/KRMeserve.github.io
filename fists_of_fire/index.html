Player 2, Choose your attack!

[i] Click 'i' on the keyboard if you want to attack. This is your basic damage dealing ability. It deals increased damage to the opponent if they are healing.

[j] Click 'j' on the keyboard to counter the enemy's attack. If the opponent attacks you, you take no damage and deal double damage back to them instead. Otherwise, this attack does nothing.

[k] Click 'k' to regroup and heal yourself. You will heal for 15% on a successful cast. If this is interrupted by an attack, you will take heavy damage instead.

[l] Click 'l' to use the special attack unique to your class. This attack will stun the enemy and skip their attack, unless both special abilities are used at the same time. You can only use this once per round.
